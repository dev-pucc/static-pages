Welcome to the DevOps Division

Where Development meets Operations to deliver Excellence!

Prerequisites

Basic Programming Knowledge

Familiarity with programming languages like Python, Java, or Shell scripting is beneficial.

Version Control

Understanding Git and collaborative development workflows is essential.

Interest in Automation

A keen interest in automating processes for efficiency and scalability.

Rules and Regulations

  • Promote collaborative and inclusive discussions.
  • Actively participate in DevOps projects and meetups.
  • Share knowledge about tools like Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, etc.
  • Avoid non-technical and irrelevant discussions during sessions.
  • Adhere to ethical practices and maintain professional decorum.
