Join the IoT and Embedded System Innovators Wing

Where devices meet intelligence and innovation takes form!

Prerequisites

Basic Electronics Knowledge

Understanding of circuits, sensors, and microcontrollers is essential.

Programming Fundamentals

Familiarity with languages like C, Python, or Arduino for IoT applications.

Passion for Innovation

A curiosity to create smarter devices and solve real-world problems.

Rules and Regulations

  • Respect all members and their ideas.
  • Actively participate in projects and discussions.
  • Focus discussions on IoT and embedded systems topics.
  • Avoid plagiarism and give proper credit to sources.
  • Adhere to club rules and project deadlines.
