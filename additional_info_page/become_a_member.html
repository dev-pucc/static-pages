Club Rules

To maintain a positive and productive environment, all members must adhere to the following rules:

  • 1. Respect Everyone: Treat all members and mentors with respect.
  • 2. Stay Professional: Use the club's resources responsibly and avoid any misuse.
  • 3. Be Inclusive: Foster a welcoming atmosphere for individuals of all backgrounds and skill levels.
  • 4. Participate Actively: Attend events, contribute to discussions, and support fellow members.
  • 5. Maintain Etiquette: Follow proper communication and collaboration practices.
  • 6. Share Knowledge: Help others by sharing your expertise and insights.
