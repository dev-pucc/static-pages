Prerequisites

Foundational Math Skills

Understanding of linear algebra, calculus, and probability is essential.

Programming Knowledge

Experience with Python and libraries like TensorFlow or PyTorch is highly recommended.

Research Enthusiasm

A strong interest in exploring AI concepts and contributing to innovative projects.

Rules and Regulations

  • Respect all members and maintain a collaborative environment.
  • Actively participate in discussions, seminars, and projects.

  • Avoid plagiarism and adhere to ethical research practices.
  • Comply with club policies and guidelines for a productive experience.
